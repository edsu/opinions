In September of 2013 the New York Times announced that 49% of Web links in Supreme Court opinions no longer worked. The article drew on the work of a team at Harvard who parsed and curated bulk data from CourtListener to determine the link rot and reference rot of URLs in SCOTUS opinions. The problem became the seed for the perma.cc web archiving project.

SCOTUS Bookmarks provides a list of URLs found in the opinion PDFs published on the SCOTUS website. The list is automatically updated and can be used by Web Archiving projects that want to ensure they have a snapshot of the context around the opinions. Suggestions and improvements for this application are welcome on GitHub. If you would like to get updates as new opinions are added please subscribe to the feed or to the mailing list.
